Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is GreenUnity?
    GreenUnity is a community-driven initiative dedicated to promoting sustainable urban agriculture and fostering vibrant communities.
  • How can I get involved?
    You can get involved by volunteering, donating, or attending our events. Visit the Get Involved tab for more information.
  • Where are your community gardens located?
    Our community gardens are located in various neighborhoods across the city. Check out our Initiatives tab for details.
  • Do I need gardening experience to participate?
    No prior gardening experience is necessary! Our workshops and resources are designed to accommodate individuals of all skill levels.
